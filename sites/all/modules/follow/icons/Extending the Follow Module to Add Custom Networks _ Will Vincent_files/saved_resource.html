          Having recently installed an update for the follow module, I would definitely suggest if you're going to do this on your site put your icons in your theme, not in the icons directory of the follow module, or you'll lose them when you install module updates... like I did.

          I've since moved my custom icons into my theme so I won't have to deal with this the next time there's an update for the follow module.
